Welcome to Climate Change Awareness

Climate change is one of the most pressing issues of our time. It refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns across the world. These changes have significant impacts on ecosystems, economies, and human health.

By raising awareness about climate change and taking action to reduce our carbon footprint, we can work towards a more sustainable future for generations to come.
